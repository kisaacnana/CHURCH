Our Mission

Our Mission

To bring people to Jesus for membership in His family, build them to maturity and train them for ministry in the Church
And send them to fulfil their mission in the world in order to magnify God's name by leading people to be devoted to Christ
Building relationship with one another and influencing their community for Christ.
