Parousia | The Journey So Far

5 years of fellowship in His presence, 5 years of Worship, 5 years of Pentecost, 5 years of The Altar Experience It’s time to Experience THE SPIRIT. You are welcome to Full Gospel Church International (FGCI). FGCI is a Charismatic Church headquartered in Tema, Ghana. In FGCI, we believe that salvation is a gift freely given by God to everyone, and our salvation is for us to serve God and humanity. In FGCI, we believe in prayer, holiness, giving, worship and evangelism. All these are aimed at transforming our lives on purpose. Join us in any of our branches across Ghana and Europe as we bring the Full Gospel of Christ to enrich your life. At FGCI, we say, evangelism is our supreme task, for transforming lives on purpose!
